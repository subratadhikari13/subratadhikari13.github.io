About me

Hi there! I'm Subrat, a student interested in interaction design. Born in Nepal, raised in Sydney, Australia.

I’m an undergraduate student at the University of Western Sydney, studying Information Systems and graduating in November 2023.

Throughout my experiences in university, I've had a particular interest in designing websites and applications. It allowed me to express my creativty and has since resulted in my curiosity to better understand human interaction and its role in creating intuitive products. I designed and coded this website, as a way for me to practice and develop my CSS/HTML and web design skills. It's become a fun side-project for me to personalise the website according to what I like. Although this current iteration of my website doesnt show any work, I plan on creating more projects to showcase on this website in the future!

Outside of university, you can see me learning photography, developing my drawing skills, rewatching Avatar the Last Airbender, and overall trying to be physically and mentally happy and healthy
